Description

This generates a prompt similar to a story from the Indiana Jones franchise

Technical

This program takes a template and chooses various prompts from a fillers container to insert within the given template.

Reflection

This is my attempt working with HTML. I wanted to have a nice atmostpheric page reminiscient of Indiana Jones, which I watched a lot when I was a kid. I had fun filling out the generator prompts too, and hopefully it makes a prompt or two that ends up being a better idea than Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom.
